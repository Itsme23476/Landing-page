Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after the 10-day free trial?

After 10 days, you can subscribe to the Pro plan for $15/month to continue using Filect. If you choose not to subscribe, the app simply stops processing new files - nothing is deleted.

Is a credit card required to start the free trial?

No. You can download Filect and start your 10-day trial immediately without entering any payment information.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. You can cancel your subscription at any point and it will remain active until the end of your billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards. Payments are processed securely through our payment provider.

Is my data safe?

Yes. File processing is handled securely via OpenAI's enterprise API. Your data is never stored by Filect and is never used to train public AI models. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
