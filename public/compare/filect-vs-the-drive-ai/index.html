What The Drive AI Does

The Drive AI reads your documents, images, and media files to understand their content. It then automatically renames files based on what's inside them and routes new files to the correct folders. The "Automatic Mode" runs in the background and handles new files as they arrive.

Its strongest feature is content-based renaming. A file called "IMG_4382.jpg" gets renamed to something like "2026-03-Kitchen-Renovation-Quote.jpg" based on what the image contains. It also transcribes audio and video files to make them searchable.

What Filect Does

Filect reads the content of every file on your drive, creates a semantic index, and lets you search everything using natural language. Type "the quote for the kitchen renovation" and find the right file in seconds. It also auto-categorizes files by topic and applies AI-generated tags.

Filect's strongest feature is search. Once your files are indexed, you can find anything by describing it. The AI matches your description to file contents by meaning, not just keywords.

Key Difference

The Drive AI is strongest at organizing files you haven't dealt with yet. New files get automatically renamed and sorted as they arrive. It's great for keeping your file system clean going forward.

Filect is strongest at finding files you already have. Thousands of existing files with bad names become instantly searchable. It's great for people who already have a messy file system and need to find things in it.
