What Sparkle Does

Sparkle watches your Desktop, Downloads, and Documents folders and automatically sorts new files into organized subfolders using AI. Drop a file on your desktop, and Sparkle figures out what it is and puts it in the right place.

It's designed to be invisible. Set it up once, and your folders stay clean without you thinking about it. No search features, no tagging, no complex interface. Just automatic cleanup.

This is perfect for non-technical Mac users who just want their desktop to stop looking like a disaster.

What Filect Does

Filect reads the content of every file on your drive, creates a semantic index, and lets you find anything by describing it in plain language. It also auto-categorizes files by topic and applies AI-generated tags. It works on both Windows and Mac.

Filect does more than organize. The main value is search. When you have 10,000 files with bad names, organization alone doesn't help. You need to be able to say "the tax document from last April" and have the right PDF appear in 2 seconds.

Key Difference

Sparkle is about keeping folders clean. It sorts new files automatically so your desktop and downloads folder don't pile up.

Filect is about finding files you can't find. It reads every file's content and lets you search by meaning. The organization is a bonus on top of powerful search.

If your problem is "my desktop is always messy," Sparkle is enough. If your problem is "I have a PDF somewhere but I can't remember what it's called," you need Filect.

Choose Sparkle if:

  • You just want your Mac folders to stay tidy
  • You prefer the simplest possible solution
  • You don't need to search inside files
  • You want a free or low-cost option

Choose Filect if:

  • You need to find files by describing their content
  • You have thousands of files with meaningless names
  • You want AI tagging and content-based organization
  • You need Windows support
  • Search is more important to you than folder cleanup
